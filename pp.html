Privacy Policy

At CodeLearn, your privacy is not an afterthought — it's a core part of how we build our platform. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control.

Your data belongs to you. We never sell your personal information to third parties. This policy is written in plain language so you always know exactly what's happening with your data.

01

Overview

CodeLearn ("we", "us", or "our") operates the CodeLearn platform — including our website, mobile applications, and all associated services (collectively, the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use our Platform.

By using CodeLearn, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to all users — students, teachers, visitors, and anyone else who interacts with our Platform. It is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms and Conditions.

02

Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve your learning experience:

Category Examples Source
Account Data Name, email address, password (hashed), role (student/teacher) You provide directly
Profile Data Profile photo, bio, learning goals, preferred language You provide directly
Learning Data Course progress, quiz scores, code submissions, certificates Generated by your activity
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session duration, click patterns Collected automatically
Device Data IP address, browser type, OS, device type, timezone Collected automatically
Payment Data Billing name, last 4 digits of card, transaction ID Via our payment processor
Communications Support tickets, forum posts, messages to instructors You provide directly

We do not collect full payment card details — these are handled directly by our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor and are never stored on our servers.

03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes, always with a lawful basis:

  • Platform delivery — Creating and managing your account, providing access to courses and features, processing payments, and issuing certificates.
  • Personalisation — Recommending relevant courses, tracking your learning streak, and adapting the experience to your skill level and goals.
  • Communication — Sending account-related emails (receipts, password resets), product updates, and — only with your consent — marketing messages.
  • Safety & security — Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access; enforcing our Terms and Conditions.
  • Analytics & improvement — Understanding how users interact with the Platform so we can fix bugs, improve features, and develop new content.
  • Legal compliance — Meeting our obligations under applicable laws and responding to lawful requests from authorities.

We will never use your data for purposes incompatible with those listed above without first obtaining your explicit consent.

04

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with carefully vetted third-party service providers who help us operate the Platform, under strict data processing agreements:

  • Payment processors — For billing and subscription management (e.g. Stripe). They process payments under their own privacy policies.
  • Cloud infrastructure — For hosting, data storage, and content delivery. All providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Analytics providers — Anonymised, aggregated usage data to help us understand platform performance.
  • Email service providers — For sending transactional and (where consented) marketing emails.
  • Customer support tools — To manage support tickets and help requests.

We may also disclose your data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CodeLearn, our users, or the public.

05

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how the Platform is used. Here's what we use and why:

Essential Cookies
Required for the Platform to function — authentication sessions, security tokens, and load balancing. These cannot be disabled.
Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how users navigate the Platform. Data is aggregated and anonymised. You can opt out in your account settings.
Functional Cookies
Remember your preferences such as language, theme, and last visited course so you pick up right where you left off.
Marketing Cookies
Only set with your explicit consent. Used to show relevant promotions. You can withdraw consent at any time via cookie settings.

You can manage cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or via our Cookie Preferences panel in your account.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law:

  • Active accounts — Data is retained for the duration of your account.
  • After account deletion — Most personal data is deleted within 30 days. Some anonymised data (e.g. aggregated usage statistics) may be retained indefinitely.
  • Financial records — Retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Legal hold — Data subject to active legal proceedings may be retained beyond standard periods.
  • Backups — Encrypted backups may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion before being purged.
07

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures, including:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a strong salt — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • Databases are encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted.
  • We operate a responsible disclosure programme — report vulnerabilities to security@codelearn.io.

While we work hard to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by law.

08

09

Children's Privacy

CodeLearn is committed to protecting the privacy of younger learners. Our Platform is available to users aged 13 and over. For users between the ages of 13 and 17, we require verifiable parental or guardian consent before account creation.

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for users under 18.
  • We do not use data from users under 18 for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • We do not allow users under 18 to make their profiles publicly visible by default.
  • Parents or guardians may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time by contacting privacy@codelearn.io.
  • If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13 without consent, we will delete it promptly.
10

International Users

CodeLearn is based in Uganda and our servers may be located in various regions worldwide. If you access our Platform from outside Uganda, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

Where we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place — such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), adequacy decisions, or other legally recognised transfer mechanisms — to ensure your data receives equivalent protection regardless of where it is processed.

11

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify you by email at least 14 days before changes take effect.
  • Display a prominent notice on the Platform.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of CodeLearn after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
